About us

When investors or traders decide to trade in the market, they use a trading system that describes how they will analyze the market and make decisions about entering or exiting positions. A trading system may include various methods of analysis, such as technical analysis, fundamental analysis, seasonal analysis, volume analysis, and others. It may also contain a set of rules for managing risks, determining position size, setting stop losses, and profit levels. The goal of a trading system is to provide the trader with a clear understanding of when to enter and exit the market and what actions to take in case of changing market conditions.
